Greetings! I am the Gotcha Novelist. Why am I called that?

You see, Gotcha Novelist is a wordplay on its own. Gotcha is a homonym of Gacha, which is a kind of toy vending machine in Japan. It has become a popular feature in most of the games I review in the Mobage section. The “Gotcha!” comes from the happy feeling that we exclaim when getting good characters from the gacha.

The second part, Novelist, stems from my hobby which is writing. I like the title “Novelist” since it somewhat represents a dream of mine. I love writing as well as playing games, so I thought—"Why not make it into an idea for a website?” And thus, GotchaNovelist was born.
