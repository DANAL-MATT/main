Genshin Impact: Character Insights in v2.2

There's been a lot of good characters that got the spotlight in Version 2.2! Let's review their roles in this version.

Childe/Tartaglia

The eleventh Fatui Harbinger— he is one of the finest warriors of the continent of Teyvat, and also one of the youngest. Personally, I see him as one of the best DPS characters in the game, boasting rapid and spreading damage when faced by a large wave of enemies.

Hu Tao

Wangsheng Funeral Parlor’s own Hu Tao, the 77th director. She specializes in risky battle styles— to maximize her damage, one needs to use her elemental skills which saps some of her health… but allows her to give much more damage based on the health that she lost!

Xinyan

The famous rocker from Liyue! After being released way back last year, she finally has the spotlight on her! Wielding a greatsword to battle, she uses both her music and her sword to deal damage to enemies— all while supporting her allies with her elemental shield!
